TechCon 2024 Conference

Unleashing Africa's Tech Power

Join us in Cairo, Egypt, for TechCon 2024, the premier conference for tech creatives, innovators, and change-makers from across Africa and around the world. This year’s theme, “Code. Create. Connect.”, celebrates the people and projects reshaping our digital future.

  • 📅 Dates: September 12–14, 2024
  • 📍 Venue: Cairo International Convention Centre

Expect hands-on workshops, visionary talks, networking lounges, product demos, and startup showcases — all in one electric space.
