About TechCon 2024

Our History

TechCon started in 2017 with a simple goal: to bring African tech minds into one space to spark ideas, grow talent, and build the future. What began as a one-day event in Nairobi has grown into an international platform, drawing thousands of attendees from across the globe.

Each year, TechCon travels to a new African city, expanding its reach and spotlighting regional innovation. From mobile technology in Lagos to fintech in Kigali, TechCon evolves with the continent’s tech landscape.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we believe technology should be accessible, inclusive, and empowering. Our mission is to inspire creative problem-solvers, equip tech talent with the tools and knowledge they need to thrive, and connect Africa’s tech scene with global opportunities. We’re building a vibrant ecosystem where innovation is celebrated, diversity is embraced, and collaboration drives progress.

Past speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed some of the most influential voices in tech. Dr. Amina Kofi, founder of CodeHub Africa, has shared insights on inclusive coding education and the future of tech talent development across the continent. Naomi Banda, CTO of SmartPay Zambia, offered powerful lessons on mobile innovation and digital finance for underserved communities. We’ve also heard from global leaders like Mikkel Thomsen, UX Director at Meta, who spoke about designing for emerging markets and championing user inclusion. These voices — and many more — continue to inspire the next generation of African tech leaders.
